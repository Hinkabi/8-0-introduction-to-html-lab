Korean Fried Chicken

Description

Korean fried chicken is on of my favorite ways to have my chicken!

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs of chicken
  • 2 Tbsp rice wine
  • 2 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 tsp fine sea salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 cup potato starch or corn starch
  • Some cooking oil for deep frying

Directions

  1. Combine ingredients in a bowl. Evenly coat the chiken
  2. In a frier add enough oil and heat it until the oil reaches 175 C / 347 F
  3. Fry them until they cook (between 3 to 5 mins, depending on the size of chicken)
  4. Take out the finished chicken and place them onto paper towels
  5. Then deep fry the chicken again when the oil temperature reaches 175 C / 347 F
  6. Fry them until the batter is golden and crisp. Only fry for 2 to 3 mins.
